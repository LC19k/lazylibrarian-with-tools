# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

---

## 📘 Do I need kepubify?  
If you use a Kobo device: **Yes**.  
If not: **No** — comment it out in the Dockerfile.

---

## 📡 Does Calibre‑Web replace Kobo Cloud?  
Only if you configure it to.  
Hybrid sync allows both to coexist.

---

## 🔌 Do I still need USB sync?  
Yes — USB sync is the only way to sync:

- Annotations  
- Highlights  
- Notes  
- Reading stats  

---

## 🔄 Do I need NickelDBus?  
If you want Wi‑Fi reading position sync: **Yes**.  
If not: optional.

---

## 📖 Do I need KOReader?  
Only if you read:

- PDFs  
- Technical books  
- Scientific papers  

For fiction: Nickel is better.

---

## 🖼 Why are my covers blurry?  
You must enable:

[✔] Generate thumbnails

In KoboTouchExtended.

---

## 🧩 Why doesn’t Kobo read series metadata?  
Kobo ignores standard EPUB metadata.  
KTE injects Kobo‑specific metadata.

---

## 🛒 Can I still buy books from the Kobo Store?  
Yes — hybrid sync preserves Kobo Store functionality.

---

## 🧠 Why use KEPUB instead of EPUB?  
KEPUB provides:

- Real page numbers  
- Faster page turns  
- Better typography  
- Kobo‑native stats  

---
